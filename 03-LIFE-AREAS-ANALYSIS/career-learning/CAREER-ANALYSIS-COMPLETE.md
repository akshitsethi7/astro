## Part 3: Profession Determination

### Step 1: Identify 10th House Planet

**Planet in 10th shows primary career nature**

**Multiple planets in 10th**:
- Strongest planet dominates
- Or combination career
- Or multiple professions

**No planet in 10th**:
- Check 10th lord placement
- Check aspects on 10th
- Check Atmakaraka (strongest planet)

### Step 2: Check 10th Lord

**10th lord placement shows career success**:

**1st house**: Self-made career, independent  
**2nd house**: Family business, wealth focus  
**3rd house**: Communication, courage-based  
**4th house**: Property, vehicles, comfort  
**5th house**: Creative, speculative, children  
**6th house**: Service, competition, health  
**7th house**: Partnership, public, foreign  
**8th house**: Research, occult, sudden changes  
**9th house**: Teaching, law, fortune, travel  
**10th house**: Strong career, focused  
**11th house**: High income, large organizations  
**12th house**: Foreign, expenses, isolation

### Step 3: Check Aspects on 10th

**Benefic aspects**: Support and success  
**Malefic aspects**: Challenges and obstacles

**Jupiter aspect on 10th**: Career protection, wisdom-based  
**Saturn aspect on 10th**: Delays but stability  
**Mars aspect on 10th**: Dynamic, competitive

### Step 4: Check Atmakaraka

**Strongest planet by degrees** (except Rahu/Ketu)

**Atmakaraka shows soul's purpose**:
- Often indicates career direction
- Shows natural talents
- Indicates life mission

### Step 5: Check D10 (Dashamsa)

**Divisional chart for career**:
- 10th house of D10
- Planets in D10
- 10th lord of D10
- Most accurate for career details

## Part 4: Business vs Service

### Service Job Indicators:

1. **Strong 6th house**
   - Planets in 6th
   - 6th lord strong
   - Service-oriented planets

2. **Saturn influence**
   - Saturn in 10th
   - Saturn aspecting 10th
   - 10th lord with Saturn

3. **Weak entrepreneurial houses**
   - Weak 2nd, 7th, 10th, 11th
   - No strong Mars/Sun

4. **Moon/Venus prominence**
   - Emotional/artistic work
   - Service to others

### Business Indicators:

1. **Strong 2nd, 7th, 10th, 11th**
   - Multiple planets
   - Lords well-placed
   - Benefic aspects

2. **Mars/Sun prominence**
   - Leadership ability
   - Independent nature
   - Risk-taking capacity

3. **Mercury strong**
   - Business acumen
   - Communication skills
   - Versatility

4. **Weak 6th house**
   - Not service-oriented
   - Independent nature

### Partnership Business:

**7th house strong**:
- Partnership ability
- Public dealings
- Collaborative work

**Venus/Mercury strong**:
- Diplomatic skills
- Communication ability
- Relationship management

## Part 5: Career Success and Recognition

### Indicators of High Success:

1. **10th house strong**
   - Benefics in 10th
   - 10th lord exalted/own sign
   - Jupiter/Venus aspect

2. **Sun strong**
   - Authority and recognition
   - Government favor
   - Leadership position

3. **Raja Yogas**
   - Kendra-Trikona lords together
   - 9th-10th lords connected
   - Multiple benefics in kendras

4. **D10 support**
   - Strong 10th in Dashamsa
   - Benefics prominent
   - No severe afflictions

### Indicators of Struggles:

1. **10th house afflicted**
   - Malefics in 10th
   - 10th lord in 6th/8th/12th
   - No benefic support

2. **Saturn/Mars affliction**
   - Delays and obstacles
   - Conflicts at work
   - Career instability

3. **Weak 10th lord**
   - Debilitated
   - Combust
   - In dusthana

4. **D10 afflictions**
   - Weak 10th in Dashamsa
   - Multiple malefics
   - No support

## Part 6: Career Timing

### When Career Starts:

**Dasha of**:
- 10th lord
- Planet in 10th
- 2nd or 11th lord
- Sun (for authority)
- Jupiter (for growth)

**Transits**:
- Jupiter transiting 10th
- Saturn transiting 10th (stabilization)
- Rahu-Ketu axis favorable

### Career Changes:

**Dasha of**:
- 8th lord (transformation)
- 12th lord (endings)
- Rahu/Ketu (sudden changes)
- Planets in 3rd (new initiatives)

**Transits**:
- Saturn changing signs
- Jupiter changing signs
- Rahu-Ketu axis shift
- Eclipses on career axis

### Peak Success Period:

**Dasha of**:
- 10th lord (if strong)
- 9th lord (luck and fortune)
- Exalted planets
- Yogakaraka planets

**Transits**:
- Jupiter in 10th or aspecting 10th
- Saturn supporting (not afflicting)
- Multiple benefic transits
