# Remedial Timing Guide

**When to start mantras, pujas, and remedies for best effect**

---

## Planetary Hora (Daily)

- **Sun hora**: Leadership, authority, government matters
- **Moon hora**: Emotions, travel, mother-related
- **Mars hora**: Courage, property, technical work
- **Mercury hora**: Communication, business, learning
- **Jupiter hora**: Wisdom, marriage, children, wealth
- **Venus hora**: Love, luxury, arts, partnership
- **Saturn hora**: Discipline, karma, long-term projects

*Start important remedies during the hora of the planet you are appeasing.*

---

## Favorable Muhurta Windows

- **Marriage-related remedies**: Jupiter hora, Venus hora; avoid Saturn hora for Venus remedies
- **Career remedies**: Sun hora, Saturn hora (for discipline)
- **Health remedies**: Moon hora, Jupiter hora
- **Wealth remedies**: Jupiter hora, Venus hora

---

## Marriage-Specific (Akshit's Chart)

- **Venus remedies** (Malavya Yoga, marriage karaka): Venus hora, Friday, Libra/Virgo transits
- **Saturn remedies** (7th lord): Saturday, Saturn hora; Saturn in 7th — maturity, not necessarily delay
- **Jupiter remedies**: Thursday, Jupiter hora — supports marriage timing

---

## General Principles

1. Start remedies on the weekday of the planet
2. Avoid eclipses and inauspicious tithis for new beginnings
3. Waxing moon (Shukla Paksha) preferred for benefic remedies
4. Waning moon (Krishna Paksha) for removal of obstacles
