## Analysis Framework

### 1. Multi-Chart System

Marriage analysis requires examining multiple divisional charts (Vargas):

**Primary Charts:**
- **D1 (Rashi)**: Overall life, personality, basic marriage indicators
- **D9 (Navamsa)**: Soul of horoscope, most important for marriage
- **D7 (Saptamsa)**: Children and progeny

**Supporting Charts:**
- **D2 (Hora)**: Wealth and financial prosperity
- **D10 (Dashamsa)**: Career and professional life
- **D60 (Shashtiamsa)**: Karmic patterns

### Why Multiple Charts?

In my 30 years of practice, I've learned that single-chart analysis is incomplete. Each divisional chart reveals a different dimension:

- D1 shows what you attract
- D9 shows what you actually get
- D7 shows what you create together

## Limitations and Disclaimers

### What Astrology Can Do

✓ Show strong tendencies and timing
✓ Reveal character and compatibility
✓ Indicate favorable and challenging periods
✓ Guide decision-making
✓ Suggest remedial measures

### What Astrology Cannot Do

✗ Guarantee exact outcomes
✗ Override free will
✗ Predict exact dates (only periods)
✗ Account for all external factors
✗ Replace personal effort

### The Role of Free Will

Astrology shows the river's flow, but you choose:
- How actively you search
- What standards you maintain
- How you respond to opportunities
- Whether you perform remedies
- How you handle challenges

### The Role of Karma

Past karma influences:
- Timing of events
- Quality of relationships
- Challenges faced
- Lessons learned

Current actions can:
- Modify timing
- Improve quality
- Reduce challenges
- Accelerate progress
