### What is Chara Dasha?
Chara Dasha is Jaimini's primary timing system for life events. It uses signs (rashi dasha) rather than planetary periods. Each sign runs for a specific number of years based on the position of its lord.

### Calculating Chara Dasha for Akshit

**Method**: Sanjay Rath's Chara Dasha (the most widely used Jaimini timing method)

**Rules for Odd Signs (Leo Lagna is odd sign):**
- Dashas start from Lagna for odd sign ascendant
- Progression is in zodiacal order (Leo → Virgo → Libra → ...)
- Duration = distance from sign lord to sign (or 12 minus that)
