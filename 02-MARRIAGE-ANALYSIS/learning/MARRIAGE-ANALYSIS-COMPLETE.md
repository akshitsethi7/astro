## Part 1: Is Marriage Promised?

### Primary Factors to Check:

**1. 7th House Analysis**
- **7th house empty + lord well-placed** = Marriage promised
- **7th house occupied by benefics** = Good marriage
- **7th house occupied by malefics** = Challenges but marriage happens
- **7th lord in 6th, 8th, or 12th** = Delays or difficulties

**2. Venus Analysis (For Men)**
- **Venus strong** = Good marriage prospects
- **Venus in good house (1, 4, 5, 7, 9, 10, 11)** = Marriage promised
- **Venus debilitated/combust** = Delays or difficulties
- **Venus in 6th, 8th, 12th** = Challenges

**3. Jupiter Analysis (For Women)**
- **Jupiter strong** = Good husband
- **Jupiter in good house** = Marriage promised
- **Jupiter debilitated/afflicted** = Husband issues
- **Jupiter in 6th, 8th, 12th** = Challenges

**4. Navamsa (D9) Chart**
- **7th house of Navamsa** = Marriage quality
- **Venus/Jupiter in Navamsa** = Spouse nature
- **Planets in 7th of D9** = Marriage characteristics

### Marriage Denial Yogas (Rare):

**1. Severe Afflictions**:
- 7th lord in 6th, 8th, or 12th + severely afflicted
- Venus (for men) or Jupiter (for women) severely debilitated + afflicted
- Multiple malefics in 7th with no benefic aspect

**2. Sannyasa Yogas**:
- Four or more planets in one house
- Specific combinations indicating renunciation
- Very rare in modern times

**Conclusion**: 95%+ charts show marriage is promised. Focus on WHEN and QUALITY.

## Part 2: Marriage Timing (K.N. Rao Method)

### Step 1: Identify Significators

**Houses to Judge**: 2nd, 7th, and 11th

**Why these houses?**
- **2nd house**: Family expansion
- **7th house**: Marriage and spouse
- **11th house**: Fulfillment of desires

### Step 2: Find Significators

**A. Planets IN these houses**:
- Any planet in 2nd, 7th, or 11th house

**B. Planets in STARS of lords of 2, 7, 11**:
- Find lords of 2nd, 7th, 11th houses
- Which planets are in their nakshatras?
- These are strong significators

**C. Lords of 2nd, 7th, 11th houses**:
- The house lords themselves

**D. Planets ASPECTING 2, 7, 11**:
- Weaker significators but still count

### Step 3: Rank Significators

**Priority Order**:
1. **Planets in nakshatras of 2, 7, 11 lords** (Strongest)
2. **Planets in 2nd, 7th, 11th houses**
3. **Lords of 2nd, 7th, 11th**
4. **Planets aspecting 2, 7, 11**

### Step 4: Apply Dasha System

**Marriage occurs in**:
- Mahadasha of significator
- Antardasha of significator
- Pratyantardasha of significator

**Best Combinations**:
- Dasha + Antara both significators
- Dasha significator + Antara strong planet
- Multiple significators active

### Step 5: Check Transits

**At time of marriage**:
- **Jupiter transiting** 7th, 1st, or aspecting 7th
- **Saturn transiting** favorably (not afflicting 7th)
- **Rahu-Ketu axis** not on 1-7 axis (or just moving away)

## Part 4: Marriage Quality and Happiness

### Factors for Happy Marriage:

**1. Benefics in 7th**:
- Jupiter, Venus, Mercury (benefic), Waxing Moon
- Bring harmony and happiness

**2. 7th Lord Strong**:
- In own sign, exalted, or friendly sign
- In good house (1, 4, 5, 7, 9, 10, 11)
- Well-aspected by benefics

**3. Venus/Jupiter Strong**:
- Not debilitated or combust
- In good houses
- Well-aspected

**4. Navamsa Support**:
- 7th house of D9 well-placed
- Venus/Jupiter strong in D9
- No severe afflictions

### Factors for Challenging Marriage:

**1. Malefics in 7th**:
- Mars (Manglik), Saturn, Rahu, Ketu, Sun
- Bring challenges and conflicts

**2. 7th Lord Weak**:
- In 6th, 8th, or 12th house
- Debilitated or combust
- Afflicted by malefics

**3. Venus/Jupiter Afflicted**:
- Debilitated, combust, or retrograde
- In dusthana houses
- Aspected by malefics

**4. Navamsa Afflictions**:
- 7th house of D9 afflicted
- Venus/Jupiter weak in D9
- Multiple malefics affecting marriage

## Part 5: Special Yogas

### Manglik Dosha (Mars in 1, 4, 7, 8, 12)

**Effects**:
- Delays in marriage
- Conflicts with spouse
- Possible separation
- Health issues to spouse

**Cancellations**:
1. Mars in own sign (Aries, Scorpio)
2. Mars exalted (Capricorn)
3. Both partners Manglik
4. Mars in 7th in movable sign
5. Jupiter aspecting Mars or 7th
6. Age above 28 (dosha weakens)

**Remedy**: Marry another Manglik or perform Mangal Shanti puja

### Kalatra Dosha (Spouse Affliction)

**Severe afflictions to 7th house**:
- Multiple malefics in 7th
- 7th lord severely afflicted
- No benefic influence

**Effects**: Serious marriage problems

**Remedy**: Strengthen Venus/Jupiter, perform specific pujas

### Divorce Yogas

**Indicators**:
1. 7th lord in 6th, 8th, or 12th + afflicted
2. Venus/Jupiter severely afflicted
3. Rahu-Ketu on 1-7 axis
4. Multiple malefics in 7th with no benefic aspect
5. 6th lord in 7th or 7th lord in 6th (litigation)

**Note**: Multiple factors needed for actual divorce

- Predict harmony level

**5. Challenges and Remedies** (10 minutes)

- Suggest specific remedies
- Provide guidance
