Malvaya Yoga is formed when Venus is in its own sign (Taurus or Libra) or in exaltation (Pisces) and occupies a Kendra house from the Ascendant or from Moon. A strong Venus that occupies a powerful position in the chart will yield more positive benefits of this yoga.  Individuals born with Malavya yoga appreciate the value of all things in their life. So they never take things for granted and enjoy even the seemingly unimportant things in life. In this chart Venus is in its own sign.

✓ Sasa Yoga
  Sasa Yoga, being one of the five of Panchmaha Purusha Yoga is a really auspicious yoga. When Saturn is in its own sign (Capricorn or Aquarius) or in its exaltation sign (Libra) and occupies Kendra house either from Ascendant or from Moon, a Sasa yoga is the result. The native of Sasa yoga loves travelling and is very appreciative of beauty and nature. They are noble and humble in all their endeavours. They are known to do well in politics. In this chart Saturn is in its own sign.
