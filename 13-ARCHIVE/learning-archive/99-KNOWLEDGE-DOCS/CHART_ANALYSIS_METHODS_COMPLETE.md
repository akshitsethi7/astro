```
Note the sub-lord of the 7th cusp.

Which houses to judge:

- 7th house: Legal bondage, partner
- 11th house: Permanent tie of friendship

How to find Significators:
1. Planets in the constellation of occupants of 2, 7, 11
2. Occupants of houses 2, 7, 11
3. Planets in constellation of lords of 2, 7, 11
4. Lords of 2, 7, 11
```

**Method: Timing Marriage**
```
Marriage will occur during the dasha-bhukti of:
- Significators of houses 2, 7, 11
- When Saturn's delay period is over (if Saturn aspects)
- During favorable sub-period of strong significator

Example: Jupiter Dasa, Jupiter Bhukti, Rahu Anthra
Date: July 14, 1965 (actual case)
```

**Method: Second Marriage**
```
Check the sub-lord of 7th cusp:
- If in dual sign → two marriages indicated
- If sub-lord of 2nd cusp connected with 7th → second marriage
- If connected with 11th → keep/affair (not legal)

Timing: Moon Dasa, Mars Bhukti, Jupiter Anthra
```

## Key Analysis Principles Extracted

### Marriage Analysis
- **Houses**: 2, 7, 11 (not just 7th)
- **Significators**: Planets in constellations of occupants/lords
- **Sub-lord**: Critical for yes/no and timing
- **Delay**: Saturn connection causes delay, not denial
- **Second Marriage**: Dual signs, 2nd cusp connection
- **Divorce**: Houses 1, 6, 10 (separation houses)

### Career Analysis
- **Houses**: 10, 2, 6 (profession, income, service)
- **Planets**: Sun (authority), Mars (technical), Mercury (business)
- **Timing**: Dasha of 10th house significators
- **Profession**: Based on strongest planet/house connection

### Wealth Analysis
- **Houses**: 2 (wealth), 11 (gains), 9 (fortune)
- **Yogas**: Dhana yogas (specific combinations)
- **Timing**: Dasha of 2nd and 11th house significators

### Dasha Analysis
- **Vimshottari**: 120-year cycle, most commonly used
- **Timing**: Events occur in dasha-bhukti-anthra of significators
- **Delay**: Malefic sub-periods delay, benefic sub-periods give results

## Example: Complete Marriage Analysis Method

### Full Analysis Procedure

**1. Check if Marriage is Promised**
- Note sub-lord of 7th cusp
- If significator of 2, 7, or 11 → Yes
- If not → Denial or extreme delay

**2. Identify Significators**
- Planets in constellation of occupants of 2, 7, 11
- Occupants of 2, 7, 11
- Planets in constellation of lords of 2, 7, 11
- Lords of 2, 7, 11

**3. Check for Delays**
- Saturn connection → Delay (not denial)
- Saturn in 5th aspecting 7th → Delay till end of Saturn period
- Saturn with Moon → Obstacles during negotiation

**4. Time the Marriage**
- Dasha of strongest significator
- Bhukti of another significator
- Anthra of third significator
- When Saturn's delay period ends

**5. Real Example**
```
Chart: Female, 30 years old
7th cusp: Sagittarius, Moon sub
Moon: Lord of 2, in 11th house
Jupiter: In 7th, in constellation of Rahu (significator of 2)
Saturn: In 6th, aspecting 7th (delay)

Analysis:
- Marriage promised (Moon sub-lord is significator)
- Delay due to Saturn aspect
- Marriage in Jupiter Dasa (after Saturn bhukti ends)

Prediction: Jupiter Dasa, Jupiter Bhukti, Rahu Anthra
Timing: After Saturn sub-period ends
```
