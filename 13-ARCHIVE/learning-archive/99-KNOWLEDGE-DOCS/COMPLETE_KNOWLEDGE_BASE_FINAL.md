Marriage promised? First of all, it should be found out whether 
the chart shows the possibility of marriage at all. According to 
Krishnamurti Padhdhati, the planet ruling the sub-division of

If the sub-lord happens to be a significator of the houses 
governing marital affairs, marriage will certainly take place.

The 7th cusp falls in Mithuna in Arudhra star owned by Rahu 
in Rahu's sub. What does Rahu denote? Rahu is posited in 
Thula very near the 11th cusp; it is in its own constellation 
Swathi and in the sub of Budha who not only rules the 
kalathra sthana (7th house) but also occupies it.

## Key Principles Extracted

### Marriage Analysis
- Houses: 2, 7, 11 (not just 7th)
- Sub-lord of 7th cusp determines if marriage promised
- Saturn connection causes delay, not denial
- Dual signs indicate second marriage
- Timing: Dasha of significators of 2, 7, 11

### Career Analysis
- Houses: 10, 2, 6
- Strongest planet/house connection shows profession
- 10th lord placement critical
- Timing: Dasha of 10th house significators

### Wealth Analysis
- Houses: 2 (wealth), 11 (gains), 9 (fortune)
- Dhana yogas: Specific planetary combinations
- 2nd and 11th lords connection important
- Timing: Dasha of 2nd and 11th significators

### Dasha Analysis
- Vimshottari: Most commonly used
- Events occur in dasha-bhukti-anthra of significators
- Malefic sub-periods delay
- Benefic sub-periods give results
