# Zodiac Signs (Rashis) - Foundation

## Introduction

The 12 zodiac signs (Rashis) are the foundation of Vedic astrology. Each sign represents 30° of the zodiac circle (360° total) and has unique characteristics that influence planets placed in them.

## The 12 Zodiac Signs

### 1. Aries (Mesha) ♈
**Degrees**: 0° - 30°  
**Element**: Fire (Agni)  
**Quality**: Movable/Cardinal (Chara)  
**Lord**: Mars  
**Exaltation**: Sun (10°)  
**Debilitation**: Saturn (20°)

**Nature**: Masculine, positive, aggressive, pioneering  
**Body Parts**: Head, brain, face  
**Direction**: East  
**Season**: Spring

**Characteristics**:
- Initiative and leadership
- Courage and boldness
- Impulsive and quick-tempered
- Independent and self-reliant
- Competitive and ambitious

**Keywords**: Action, courage, leadership, independence, aggression

## Sign Classifications

### By Element (Tattva)

**Fire Signs (Agni)**: Aries, Leo, Sagittarius
- Energy, action, enthusiasm
- Leadership, courage, initiative
- Impulsive, aggressive, passionate

**Earth Signs (Prithvi)**: Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn
- Stability, practicality, materialism
- Grounded, reliable, patient
- Stubborn, slow, conservative

**Air Signs (Vayu)**: Gemini, Libra, Aquarius
- Intellect, communication, social
- Versatile, adaptable, mental
- Detached, indecisive, restless

**Water Signs (Jala)**: Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces
- Emotion, intuition, sensitivity
- Nurturing, psychic, deep
- Moody, secretive, escapist

### By Quality (Guna)

**Movable/Cardinal (Chara)**: Aries, Cancer, Libra, Capricorn
- Initiative, action, change
- Leadership, pioneering
- Restless, impatient

**Fixed (Sthira)**: Taurus, Leo, Scorpio, Aquarius
- Stability, persistence, determination
- Stubborn, resistant to change
- Reliable, enduring

**Dual/Mutable (Dvisvabhava)**: Gemini, Virgo, Sagittarius, Pisces
- Adaptability, flexibility, versatility
- Dual nature, indecisive
- Changeable, adjustable

### By Gender

**Masculine/Positive**: Aries, Gemini, Leo, Libra, Sagittarius, Aquarius
- Active, outgoing, assertive
- Yang energy

**Feminine/Negative**: Taurus, Cancer, Virgo, Scorpio, Capricorn, Pisces
- Receptive, inward, passive
- Yin energy

## Sign Relationships

### Friendly Signs
- Fire signs friendly with Fire and Air
- Earth signs friendly with Earth and Water
- Air signs friendly with Air and Fire
- Water signs friendly with Water and Earth

### Enemy Signs
- Fire signs enemy with Water
- Earth signs enemy with Air
- Air signs enemy with Water
- Water signs enemy with Fire

### Neutral Signs
- Adjacent signs are generally neutral

## Practical Application

### In Chart Reading

1. **Ascendant Sign**: Personality, physical body, life approach
2. **Moon Sign**: Mind, emotions, inner self
3. **Sun Sign**: Soul, ego, father, authority
4. **Planetary Placements**: Each planet's expression modified by sign

### Sign Strength

**Strong Placement**:
- Own sign (Svakshetra)
- Exaltation (Uchcha)
- Moolatrikona
- Friendly sign (Mitra)

**Weak Placement**:
- Debilitation (Neecha)
- Enemy sign (Shatru)
- Combust (too close to Sun)

### Interpretation Tips

1. **Consider the sign lord**: Where is the sign's ruler placed?
2. **Check aspects**: What planets aspect the sign?
3. **Analyze house**: Which house does the sign occupy?
4. **Evaluate strength**: Is the planet strong or weak in this sign?
5. **Synthesize**: Combine sign, planet, house, aspects

## Classical References

### From Brihat Parashara Hora Shastra (BPHS)
> "The signs are the vehicles of planets. A planet in own sign is like a king in his palace, in exaltation like a king visiting another kingdom with honor, in debilitation like a king in prison."

### From Jataka Parijata
> "The nature of the sign modifies the expression of the planet. Fire signs give energy, Earth signs give stability, Air signs give intellect, Water signs give emotion."
