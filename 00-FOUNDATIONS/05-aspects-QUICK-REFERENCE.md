# Planetary Aspects (Drishti) - Quick Reference Guide

**How planets influence each other across the chart**

---

## What Are Aspects?

Aspects (Drishti = "glance") are the way planets influence houses and other planets from a distance. Unlike Western astrology, Vedic astrology uses specific aspect rules based on house positions.

**Key Concept**: Planets don't just affect their own house - they "aspect" (cast their influence on) other houses and planets.

---

## Basic Aspect Rules

### Universal 7th House Aspect

**ALL planets aspect the 7th house from themselves**

**Example**:
- Planet in 1st house → aspects 7th house
- Planet in 3rd house → aspects 9th house
- Planet in 10th house → aspects 4th house

**Why 7th?**: Represents opposition, partnership, completion

---

## Special Aspects

### Mars (Mangal) - The Warrior
**Aspects**: 4th, 7th, and 8th houses from itself

**Example**: Mars in 1st house aspects:
- 4th house (home, mother)
- 7th house (marriage, partnerships)
- 8th house (transformation, obstacles)

**Nature**: Aggressive, energetic, can cause conflicts
**Effect**: Brings energy, action, sometimes aggression

---

### Jupiter (Guru) - The Teacher
**Aspects**: 5th, 7th, and 9th houses from itself

**Example**: Jupiter in 1st house aspects:
- 5th house (children, intelligence)
- 7th house (marriage, partnerships)
- 9th house (luck, father, dharma)

**Nature**: Benefic, protective, expansive
**Effect**: Brings wisdom, protection, growth

---

### Saturn (Shani) - The Disciplinarian
**Aspects**: 3rd, 7th, and 10th houses from itself

**Example**: Saturn in 1st house aspects:
- 3rd house (courage, siblings)
- 7th house (marriage, partnerships)
- 10th house (career, status)

**Nature**: Restrictive, disciplining, delaying
**Effect**: Brings discipline, delays, responsibilities

---

## Aspect Strength

### Full Aspects (100%)
- 7th house aspect (all planets)
- Special aspects (Mars, Jupiter, Saturn)

### Partial Aspects (Varies)
Some astrologers use:
- 5th and 9th house aspects (75%)
- 4th and 8th house aspects (75%)
- 3rd and 10th house aspects (50%)

**Note**: Classical texts primarily use full aspects only

---

## How Aspects Work

### Benefic Aspects
**Jupiter, Venus, Mercury (when benefic), Waxing Moon**

**Effects**:
- Protect the aspected house
- Improve results
- Bring positive outcomes
- Support and nourish

**Example**: Jupiter in 5th aspecting 9th house
- Enhances luck and fortune
- Good for father
- Spiritual growth
- Higher education

### Malefic Aspects
**Saturn, Mars, Sun, Rahu, Ketu, Waning Moon**

**Effects**:
- Create challenges
- Cause delays or obstacles
- Bring transformation
- Test and strengthen

**Example**: Saturn in 10th aspecting 4th house
- Delays in property
- Mother may face challenges
- Emotional restrictions
- But brings discipline to home

---

## Aspect Combinations

### Multiple Benefic Aspects
**Best scenario**
- House receives aspects from Jupiter, Venus, Mercury
- Strong protection
- Excellent results
- Multiple blessings

### Multiple Malefic Aspects
**Challenging scenario**
- House receives aspects from Saturn, Mars, Rahu
- Significant challenges
- Requires remedies
- Tests and obstacles

### Mixed Aspects
**Most common**
- Both benefic and malefic aspects
- Balanced results
- Some challenges, some support
- Realistic outcomes

**Jupiter aspects**:

**Mars aspects**:
- 6th house (enemies, health)
- 9th house (luck, dharma)
- 10th house (career)

**No direct aspect on 5th house (children)**
- Mars doesn't directly affect children

**Chart**: Jupiter in 9th house

- 1st house (self) ← Protects personality

- But Jupiter's general beneficence helps
- 1st house aspect strengthens overall chart

## Aspect Analysis Checklist

### For Each House, Check:

1. **Which planets are IN the house?**
   - Direct influence (strongest)

2. **Which planets ASPECT the house?**
   - Indirect influence (important)

3. **Are aspects benefic or malefic?**
   - Determines nature of results

4. **How many aspects?**
   - Multiple aspects = stronger influence

5. **What is the house lord doing?**
   - House lord's placement matters most

## Special Aspect Scenarios

### Aspect on Lagna (1st House)
**Very Important**
- Affects entire personality
- Influences health and vitality
- Shapes life direction

**Benefic aspects**: Strong personality, good health  
**Malefic aspects**: Health challenges, personality issues

### Aspect on 7th House
**Critical for Marriage**
- All planets aspect 7th from 1st
- Mars, Jupiter, Saturn have special aspects to 7th
- Multiple aspects common

**Benefic aspects**: Happy marriage  
**Malefic aspects**: Marriage challenges

### Aspect on 10th House
**Career Impact**
- Saturn aspects 10th from 1st (important!)
- Jupiter aspects 10th from 2nd
- Mars aspects 10th from 3rd

**Benefic aspects**: Career success  
**Malefic aspects**: Career struggles

## Conjunction vs Aspect

### Conjunction (Planets in Same House)
- **Strongest influence**
- Planets blend energies
- Direct interaction
- Most powerful

### Aspect (Planets in Different Houses)
- **Moderate influence**
- Planets influence from distance
- Indirect interaction
- Important but less powerful than conjunction

**Priority**: Conjunction > Aspect > House lordship

## Common Aspect Patterns

### Pattern 1: Mutual Aspect
**Two planets aspect each other**
- Creates strong connection
- Mutual influence
- Important relationship

**Example**: Planet in 1st and planet in 7th
- Both aspect each other
- Strong mutual influence

### Pattern 2: One-Way Aspect
**One planet aspects another, not vice versa**
- One-directional influence
- Aspecting planet dominates

**Example**: Jupiter in 5th aspects 9th
- But planet in 9th doesn't aspect 5th back
- Jupiter's influence is one-way

### Pattern 3: Multiple Aspects on One House
**Several planets aspect same house**
- House becomes very important
- Mixed influences
- Complex results

**Example**: 7th house aspected by Mars, Saturn, Jupiter
- Marriage is major life theme
- Mixed influences (challenges + blessings)
