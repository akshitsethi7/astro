# The 27 Nakshatras - Quick Reference Guide

**Lunar mansions for precise timing and character analysis**

---

## What Are Nakshatras?

Nakshatras are 27 lunar mansions, each spanning 13°20' of the zodiac. They provide subtle, precise information about personality, timing, and life events. The Moon travels through one nakshatra per day.

**Key Concept**: While signs show general nature and houses show life areas, nakshatras reveal subtle characteristics and precise timing.

---

## Why Nakshatras Matter

1. **Vimshottari Dasha**: Calculated from birth nakshatra
2. **Personality**: Reveals subtle character traits
3. **Compatibility**: Essential for marriage matching
4. **Timing**: Precise event timing (muhurta)
5. **Remedies**: Specific to nakshatra lord

## Marriage Compatibility (Kuta System)

**8 Kutas (Compatibility Factors)**:
1. **Varna** (1 point) - Spiritual compatibility
2. **Vashya** (2 points) - Mutual attraction
3. **Tara** (3 points) - Health and well-being
4. **Yoni** (4 points) - Sexual compatibility
5. **Graha Maitri** (5 points) - Mental compatibility
6. **Gana** (6 points) - Temperament
7. **Bhakoot** (7 points) - Love and affection
8. **Nadi** (8 points) - Health of progeny

**Total**: 36 points  
**Minimum for marriage**: 18 points  
**Good match**: 24+ points  
**Excellent match**: 30+ points

**Note**: Nadi dosha (same nadi) is serious - avoid unless cancelled

## Nakshatra Padas (Quarters)

Each nakshatra has 4 padas (quarters) of 3°20' each:
- **Pada 1**: First navamsa
- **Pada 2**: Second navamsa
- **Pada 3**: Third navamsa
- **Pada 4**: Fourth navamsa

**Example**: Ashwini
- Pada 1: 0°00'-3°20' Aries (Aries navamsa)
- Pada 2: 3°20'-6°40' Aries (Taurus navamsa)
- Pada 3: 6°40'-10°00' Aries (Gemini navamsa)
- Pada 4: 10°00'-13°20' Aries (Cancer navamsa)
