## About

Deva Keralam (Chandrakala Nadi) is one of the most exhaustive textbooks of Nadi astrology.
It covers predictions based on Nadiamsa positions across all signs, covering topics like
dashas, planets in houses, marriage, children, wealth, health, career, and remedies.
